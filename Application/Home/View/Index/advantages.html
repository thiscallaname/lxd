Welding

First, advanced technology and facility

We use imported German automatic laser welding machine to ensure 100% penetration rate of fin and base tube.Higher the penetration rate, higher the heat exchange efficiency is.Metallographic analysis is shown right:

Second, high performance, light weight

With the minimum base tube wall thickness(1.0mm) and the minimum fin thickness(0.4mm), our welding process can greatly reduce the thermal resistance. The unique fin winding process ensures the minimum spacing between fins of 2.0 mm. Beside, the fin of our products is evenly distributed and smooth at the weld witch prevent the accumulation of ash. With all these advanced processes, we improved the heat-exchange rate of our products by a wide margin. Thus,with the same specifications, our energy-efficient laser welded finned tubes are more efficient than high-frequency welded finned tubes by 30% or more.
