About Us

Established in 2010, Suzhou Aier Mart Energy Saving Technology Co., Ltd is a manufacturer and trader specialized in the research, development and production of all types of energy-efficient laser welded finned tubes. Our production lines are equipped with advanced facilities and apply with German advanced technologies. All our products comply with international quality standards and are certified by CE, FCC, ROSS etc.

The main products are all kinds of energy-efficient laser welded finned tubes which used in many fields both at home and abroad, such as petroleum and petrochemical industry, power industry, solar industry, mechanical and electrical equipment production, pharmaceutical industry, food industry, ship industry etc. Also we have spiral-type tube bending machine as our patented product.
